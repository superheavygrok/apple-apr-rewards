The mechanic

5% tax. 100% to buybacks.

Every $APR transaction routes 5% to a buyback wallet that swaps SOL into $APPLE on Jupiter, then distributes that $APPLE to $APR holders. The price pressure on $APPLE flows directly to the Arctic Fox Daily Rescue charity wallet on donate.gg.

Arctic Fox Daily Rescue

Meet Apple. A baby fox born with hydrocephalus.

$APR exists because of one fox. Apple was born with hydrocephalus — a build-up of fluid on the brain that, for most rescued foxes, ends in euthanasia. Kimberly, founder of Arctic Fox Daily Rescue, refused. Apple is now in round-the-clock specialist care, funded by the $APPLE charity coin on donate.gg.

$APR is the rewards layer on top of that. Every $APR trade buys back $APPLE — boosting the charity coin's price, growing its raises, and putting more $APPLE in your wallet at the same time.
